Fame Parties eCommerce and Photography Gallery

Product Genre: eCommerce Store

Technology Tools: WordPress/PHP/MySQL, Woocommerce, jQuery, HTML5, CSS

Sara's Role: Lead Developer and Product Designer

Team Members: 2 Developers, 1 Intern Developer, 1 Graphic/UX Designer

The Project: Fame Parties had been doing all their website hosting and design using online resources and some family support. They had become frustrated with the limited design and functionality found in Saas products such as Squarespace. They also wanted to have a custom product builder functionality as well as a photo gallery, each with payment portals. Since the website might host up to 500 new images daily, we also had to have an S3 API connection, so as to not fill up the database memory allocation in about a month.

The team also built a customized UX product builder, allowing customers to apply their own images to products to add a personalized touch to increase the ROI of the cards.

The Product:Â Fame Parties was very interested in a high quality design and high customizability and interested in seeing a great UX on the product designer. We put together a 6 layer solution to get precise UX that let the user swap out images, but only images in a certain layer of the stack, keeping all other layers intact. This was tricky and took about 5 different iterations on the Product Designer until we had our final product.

Modern Submissions Small Business Website

Product Genre: Small business advertising website including media package

Web Technology Tools: WordPress/PHP/MySQL, jQuery, React, HTML/CSS

Marketing Tools: Google Analytics, MailChimp, Pipedrive

My Role: Lead Developer and Head Project Manager

Team Members: 2 Developers, 2 Graphic Designers/Videographers, 1 Creative Director and Copywriter

The Project: The client, Michael, had built a React app that solved a large pain point in the Insurance Industry. While building his product, he was also doing sales, marketing, customer support and product feature writing on his own. After feeling dissatisfied with his work on his website and marketing materials, Michael turned to Left Turn Only in partnership with Swizzle to put together an improved forward facing, cohesive brand presence online.

 

The Products: Under my project management, we built a new website in WordPress, complete with new copy, images, design, and custom pages embedded with components from the React product.
